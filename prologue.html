+ in my dreams i can fly +

I swim through the night sky and bask in the light of the glimmering stars.
In the mornings I can soar between the rays of the sun,
and I can dance above the wisps of clouds
for the wind takes me higher than the dreams of Imagination.

+ in my dreams i am falling +

But when I look down, there are shackles dragging me down.
The wind has shifted and the sun is no longer radiant.
It is harsh and fiery — burning everything that angers it.
Now I am falling... falling... falling...

+ in my dreams i am trapped +

I am stuck — a prisoner to the winds that once brought me life.
A prisoner to the sun that once brought me warmth.
They are chains and wardens: pulling me in and dragging me down.
When I retaliate, they leave me scarred and disfigured.

+ in my dreams i am strong +

In my dreams, I dream of the stars.
I dream to be free and unbound to the world.
To fly and again feel weightless.
but I open my eyes to walls so instead I remain — simply enduring.

+ this is my writing that is for no one at all +
